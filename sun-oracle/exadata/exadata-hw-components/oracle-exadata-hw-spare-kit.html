Oracle Exadata Spares Kit Components

  • FR – Full Rack

  • HR – Half Rack

  • QR – Quarter Rack

  • ER – Eighth Rack

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X11M

Note: No spares kit is included with Oracle Exadata Database Machine X11M. Spare hard disk drives and flash devices may be purchased separately.

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X10M

Note: No spares kit is included with Oracle Exadata Database Machine X10M. Spare hard disk drives and flash devices may be purchased separately.

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X9M-2 and X9M-8

  • 1 x 18 TB disk for High Capacity storage servers only

  • 1 x 6.4 TB NVMe PCI Flash card

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X8M-2, X8M-8, X8-2, and X8-8

  • 1 x 14 TB SAS disk for High Capacity storage servers only

  • 1 x Flash Accelerator F640 or F640v2 PCIe card

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X7-2 and X7-8

  • 1 x 10 TB 7.2k RPM SAS disk for High Capacity storage servers only

  • 1 x Flash Accelerator F640 PCIe card

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X6-2

  • 1 x 8 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disk

  • 1 x flash accelerator F320 PCIe card for high capacity storage servers only

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X5-2

  • 1 x 8 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disk

  • 1 x flash accelerator F160 PCIe card for high capacity storage servers only

  • 1 x 1.6 TB solid-state drive for extreme flash storage servers only

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X5-8 and X6-8

  • 1 x 8 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disk

  • 1 x flash accelerator F160 PCIe card for high capacity storage servers only

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X4-2

  • 1 x 1.2 TB 10k RPM High Performance SAS disks or 1 x 4 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disks

  • 1 x 800 GB Exadata Smart Flash Cache card

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X4-8

  • 1 x 1.6 TB NVMe PCIe 3.0 High Performance or 1 x 4 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disks

  • 1 x flash accelerator F160 PCIe card for high capacity storage servers only

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X3-2 (FR)

  • 2 x 600 GB 15k RPM High Performance SAS disks or 2 x 3 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disks

  • 2 x 400 GB Exadata Smart Flash Cache cards

  • 6 x 3 m, and 10 x 5 m InfiniBand cables

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X3-2 (HR)

  • 1 x 600 GB 15k RPM High Performance SAS disks or 1 x 3 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disks

  • 1 x 400 GB Exadata Smart Flash Cache card

  • 6 x 3 m, and 10 x 5 m InfiniBand cables

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X3-2 (QR, ER)

  • 1 x 600 GB 15k RPM High Performance SAS disks or 1 x 3 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disks

  • 1 x 400 GB Exadata Smart Flash Cache card

  • 4 x 5 m InfiniBand cables

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X3-8 (FR) with X4-2L Storage Servers

  • 1 x 1.2 TB 10k RPM High Performance SAS disks or 1 x 4 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disks

  • 1 x 400 GB Exadata Smart Flash Cache card

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X3-8 (FR) with X3-2 Storage Servers

  • 2 x 600 GB 15k RPM High Performance SAS disks or 2 x 3 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disks

  • 2 x 400 GB Exadata Smart Flash Cache cards

  • 6 x 3 m, and 10 x 5 m InfiniBand cables

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X2-2 and X2-8 (FR)

  • 2 x 600 GB 15k RPM High Performance SAS disks or 2 x 3 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disks

  • 2 x 96 GB Exadata Smart Flash Cache cards

  • 6 x 3 m, and 10 x 5 m InfiniBand cables

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X2-2 and X2-8 (HR)

  • 1 x 600 GB 15k RPM High Performance SAS disks or 1 x 3 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disks

  • 1 x 96 GB Exadata Smart Flash Cache card

  • 6 x 3 m, and 10 x 5 m InfiniBand cables

Oracle Exadata Database Machine X2-2 and X2-8 (QR)

  • 1 x 600 GB 15k RPM High Performance SAS disks or 1 x 3 TB 7.2k RPM High Capacity SAS disks

  • 1 x 96 GB Exadata Smart Flash Cache card

  • 4 x 5 m InfiniBand cables
